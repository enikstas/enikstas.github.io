How Do You Get Your Business Insights?

Off-the-shelf software gives you rigid tables. Custom dashboards give you control.

Standard Software Reports Limit Your Vision

  • Rigid Templates: You cannot add custom calculations or view data across locations the way your business actually operates.
  • Manual Copy-Paste: Exporting CSV files daily to match sales with ad spend takes hours of repetitive work.
  • Lagging Data: Decisions are made based on last month's stats instead of real-time insights.
  • Disjointed Software: Cash registers, inventory tracking, QuickBooks, and Shopify are siloed and don't talk to each other.

Custom Automated Dashboards Enable Growth

  • Built Around Your Business: Custom metrics designed to show exactly what drives profit for your specific setup.
  • Complete Integration: Automatically consolidates sales data, marketing costs, and inventory under a single link.
  • Real-time Analytics: Updates automatically. Open your dashboard on your phone or laptop and see today's numbers instantly.
  • Actionable Highlights: Flag anomalies, low-margin products, and customer retention opportunities automatically.
